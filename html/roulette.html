How to play:

  • You will begin with $100 (virtual) to bet
  • There are 3 different kinds of bets with increasingly higher returns.
    1. Bet on color: guess if it will land on red or black. This bet is 1-1, which means you will win as much as you bet (ex. if you bet say, $100, and get it right, you will win that much virtual money)
    2. Bet on group of 12: guess which group of 12 the ball will land (1-12, 13-24, 25-36). This bet is 2-1, meaning you win double the amount you bet.
    3. Bet on single number: guess which exact number it will land on. This bet is 20-1, meaning upon guessing the correct value, you will win 20 times that value.
  • Note: if you guess incorrectly, the amount you put in to bet will be subtracted from your current amount.
  • You lose if you run out of money

Good luck!
